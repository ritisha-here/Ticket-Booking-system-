About Us

The goal of this website is to serve as the ultimate resource for discovering and exploring the rich array of museums in India. We aim to provide comprehensive and up-to-date information on the diverse range of museums that showcase the city’s art, history, science, and culture. Whether you’re a resident, a tourist, or a scholar, our site is designed to help you navigate the vibrant museum scene in India, offering insights into each institution’s unique offerings, exhibitions, and events.
